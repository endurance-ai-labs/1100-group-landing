08 — Trust by construction

If it can't cite it, it won't claim it.

The 1100 Brain is built around a single hard rule: every fact must trace to a real source. It rejects any synthesized claim that isn't backed by a link to the underlying ticket, reservation, review or report.

Born from a real failure mode. Generic AI tools state confident answers with zero evidence. The citation rule means that can never happen silently.

When the data is thin, the Brain says so. Rather than filling the gap with confident fiction.

Every answer is one click from the source. The Toast ticket, the OpenTable booking, the Yelp review it came from.
